# Welcome to DKU Wiki Project!

You can find our issue at [wiki.dkucs.com](wiki.dkucs.com).

## Contribution Guide

All contributions must be made through a GitHub **[pull request](https://docs.github.com/en/pull-requests/collaborating-with-pull-requests/proposing-changes-to-your-work-with-pull-requests/about-pull-requests)** (PR) or **[Issues](https://docs.github.com/en/issues/tracking-your-work-with-issues/using-issues/creating-an-issue#creating-an-issue-from-a-repository)**. We recommend each contribution focus on one issue or one article only. All activity must follow our community [CODE_OF_CONDUCT](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)

### Contribution by Issue

1. Go to our [issues page](https://github.com/DKU-CS-Club/wiki.dkucs.com/issues).
2. [Create a issue](https://docs.github.com/en/issues/tracking-your-work-with-issues/using-issues/creating-an-issue#creating-an-issue-from-a-repository) with the information you want to contribute.
3. That's it! Thank you for your contribution.

### Contribution by Forking

1. [Fork](https://docs.github.com/en/pull-requests/collaborating-with-pull-requests/working-with-forks/fork-a-repo) this repo.
2. [Clone](https://docs.github.com/en/repositories/creating-and-managing-repositories/cloning-a-repository) your fork on your own machine.
3. Make changes with an editor of you choice (Such as VS code, NeoVim, Nano, Text Editors, etc).
4. [Add](https://github.com/git-guides/git-add),[Commit](https://github.com/git-guides/git-commit), [Push](https://docs.github.com/en/get-started/using-git/pushing-commits-to-a-remote-repository) your changes.
5. Create a [Pull Request](https://docs.github.com/en/pull-requests/collaborating-with-pull-requests/proposing-changes-to-your-work-with-pull-requests/creating-a-pull-request-from-a-fork) to this repository.
6. Ask someone on the team to [review](https://docs.github.com/en/pull-requests/collaborating-with-pull-requests/proposing-changes-to-your-work-with-pull-requests/requesting-a-pull-request-review) your pull request.
7. That's it! Thank you for your contribution.

Bug reports and issue submissions are welcome!
